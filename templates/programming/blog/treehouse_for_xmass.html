Team Treehouse:

There are lots of free online tutorials; however, most such tutorials lack structure and/or direction. Think about math in grade school, your second grade teach talked with your first grade teacher, knew what you had learned and knew your general level of education. Thus, first grade math logically flowed into second grade math. Many online tutorials lack this synchronization: Courses are disjointed, either overlapping too much providing redundant information or (worse) exist too far apart. The student gains island of knowledge that don't really work together.

Enter Team Treehouse. There are a number of tracks designed to avoid the above mentioned problems. Lots of short videos are combined in a logical way to build the student towards a final product. The basic "Web Design" track teaches the viewer to (starting with assumed zero experience) build a simple static website while tackling each individual hurtle separately and logically. Though it costs 25$ / mo, I think it will be worth it if they use it. Shameless plug: If you have any desire to try it out, we can both get cheaper subscriptions if you use my referral code here!
